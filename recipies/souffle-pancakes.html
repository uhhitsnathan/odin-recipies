The All-Father's Souffle Pancake Recipie

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 tablespoon whole milk
  • .25 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • .25 cup cake flour
  • .5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon neutral-flavored oil
  • 2 tablespoon water(for steaming)

Instructions

Step 1

Place egg whites in a bowl or stand mixer. Place egg yolks into a large bowl, then add milk and vanilla and whisk until nice and frothy, like a good ol' Giants Ale from Blue Cloth Tavern and Inn. Then, sift the cake flour and baking powder into the mixture, whisk again, and set aside.
