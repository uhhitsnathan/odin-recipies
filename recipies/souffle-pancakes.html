The All-Father's Souffle Pancake Recipie

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 tablespoon whole milk
  • .25 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • .25 cup cake flour
  • .5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon neutral-flavored oil
  • 2 tablespoon water(for steaming)

Instructions

Step 1

Place egg whites in a bowl or stand mixer. Place egg yolks into a large bowl, then add milk and vanilla and whisk until nice and frothy. Then, sift the cake flour and baking powder into the mixture, whisk again, and set aside.

Step 2

Freeze the egg whites for at least 15 minutes, and then begin to beat with hand mixer. Mix until whites turn frothy and pale, then gradually add in the sugar; keep beating until egg whites form peaks and are stiff.

Step 3

Heat a large frying pan to 150U+000B0 C, and grease with oil. Add egg whites to yolk mixture, gently folding in to not break air bubbles. Add dollops of mixture to heated pan.

Step 4

Set timer for 6 minutes, then add water to pan and cover with a lid. Steam, then flip, and repeat for the other side. Pancake sides should be lightly browned, but cooked all the way through.

Step 5

Enjoy a delicously fluffly pancake with powdered sugar, whipped cream, and fresh fruit!
