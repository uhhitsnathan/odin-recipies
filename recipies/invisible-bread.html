Invisible Bread

A quick snack, whether it be breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Nutrition Facts

  1. None, because the bread does not exist.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of air
  • 1 tablespoon of invisible yeast
  • 3 cups of warmed invisible water
  • Invisible bowl, invisible rolling pin
  • 2 cups of invisible flour

Instructions

Step 1

Begin by adding all ingredients to a large size invisible bowl. All ingredients must be added in gradually in order to not mess up the invisibleness of the dish.

Step 2

Slowly mix all ingrediants together, until mixture is no longer sticky. Then, flour your invisible surfact and your invisible rolling pin, and begin stretching out the invisible dough.

Step 3

Oil an invisible bowl, add dough, cover and let rest 30 mins, then uncover, press down on dough, and repeat once more. Then, turn off your oven, as invisible bread does not need heat to cook.

Step 4

Put bread in oven, and leave it for 5 seconds, take out, and enjoy your fresh loaf of non-existant bread!
